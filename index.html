Luigi is Mario's clumsy and often fear prone younger brother.

Princess Peach is the ruler and protector of the Mushroom Kingdom. At first, she has always been prone to being kidnapped by Bowser,
but as the series went on, she has become more and more independent and capable of taking enemies out on her own, as demonstrated in a few of her standalone games.
As the princess of the Mushroom Kingdom and one of Mario's strongest allies, she is always ready to lend Mario a helping hand!

Bowser is the tyrannical king of the koopas and the archenemy of the Super Mario Bros.
He has come up with countless ways to try and conquer the Mushroom Kingdom, sometimes even the world, and occasionall even the whole galaxy.
Luckily, Mario is always ready to kick his tail and knock him all the way back to his castle!

Bowser Jr. is the son of Bowser and one of Mario's recurring antagonists.
He is often seen trying to follow in his father's footsteps and cause trouble for Mario and his friends, but he is also shown to have a softer side and a desire to be accepted by his father.
Despite his mischievous nature, Bowser Jr. is a complex character who adds depth to the Super Mario Bros. series and is always ready to cause trouble for Mario and his friends!
